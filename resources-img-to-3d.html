Best Practices for Uploading Images

To get the most accurate and high-quality 3D results, we suggest using a clean, front-facing image of the object you'd like to convert. A plain white or solid-colored background with no extra elements will help Bevelify generate a more precise model from your image.

    Ideal Image Guidelines:
  • ✅ One object per image
  • ✅ Clear front-facing view
  • ✅ White or solid-color background with minimal distractions
    What to Avoid:
  • ⚠️ Images with text or labels
  • ⚠️ Photos containing multiple objects
  • ⚠️ Busy or detailed backgrounds
  • ❌ Any inappropriate, offensive, or NSFW content

Keeping your input image simple and focused helps Bevelify bring your 3D vision to life with clarity and detail.

Style Your 3D Models with Bevelify’s Creative Modes

Bevelify offers two distinct styling options to elevate the quality of your 3D models. Whether you're going for artistic depth or realistic finishes, these modes help enhance your output with precision.

Sculpture Mode: This style generates high-resolution models featuring baked textures, displacement maps, and ambient occlusion for a sculpted, lifelike appearance. Perfect for projects that demand fine detail and photogrammetry-level realism.

PBR Mode: Short for Physically Based Rendering, this mode automatically enhances your models with realistic lighting, surface textures, and depth—ideal for adding richness and detail to your 3D objects effortlessly.

Choose the style that best suits your creative vision and let Bevelify do the heavy lifting.
